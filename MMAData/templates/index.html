UFC Fighter Database

Introduction

Welcome to my site! This site is meant for the viewing and downloading of the UFC fighter database. In addition to the statistics kept by the UFC on each fighter, I also have created my own variable called Classification. A summary of all variables is included below. The source of all the data can be found here.

Variables/Columns Description

  • Name: Name of an individual UFC fighter
  • Wins: Number of wins in professional MMA bouts
  • Losses: Number of losses in professional MMA bouts
  • Draws: Number of draws in professional MMA bouts
  • Height: Height of an individual UFC fighter (inches)
  • Weight: Weight of an individual UFC fighter (pounds)
  • Reach: Reach of an individual UFC fighter (inches)
  • Stance: Stance of an individual UFC fighter
  • DOB: Date of birth of an individual UFC fighter
  • SLpM: Significant Strikes Landed per Minute
  • StrAcc: Significant Striking Accuracy (percent)
  • SApM: Significant Strikes Absorbed per Minute
  • StrDef: Significant Strike Defence (the % of opponents strikes that did not land)
  • TDAvg: Average Takedowns Landed per 15 minutes
  • TDAcc: Takedown Accuracy (percent)
  • TDDef: Takedown Defense (the % of opponents TD attempts that did not land)
  • SubAvg: Average Submissions Attempted per 15 minutes
  • Classification: Classifies a fighter based on their style. There are 5 possible styles: Heavy Striking, Light Striking, Balanced, Light Grappling, and Heavy Grappling. The words “heavy” and “light” refers to the quantity of attributes. For example, a fighter with a Heavy Striking style much more often strikes than grapples.
